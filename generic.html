Definition of Fire servers

Fire servers are servers that do not have any followers of the policy of a particular country or the language of preview but the majority in this society are Arabs and some foreigners and these servers are characterized in their continuous support and desire to achieve a unified goal which is to gain experience and enlarge their branches

Fire branches are divided into three branches

Fire developer: A community of programmers is the main branch and features the sale of software services or anything under the technological theme and also features in the education of open source code languages for free

Fire Designer: The second branch of the branches is a branch dedicated to the art of technology and it is the design of images and videos and all the works of modern art in modern technological machinery

Fire Communty: A branch of ours which is dedicated to public chat, competitions and fun time with friends

Fire has no manager and no official open to the whole world.
